About Us

Crown is a student-led FTC robotics team founded in 2024. We are passionate about engineering, competition, and collaboration.

Our Creations

EXCALIBUR I

Advanced stainless steel intake system optimized for precision and durability.

EXCALIBUR II

Iterative refinement with dual-arm modularity and optimized claw intake.

Our Collaborations

SITHS SATURN

Collaboration on advanced drivetrain concepts with Siths Saturn, exchanging insights on CAD design, differential systems, and high-traction maneuverability.

HAZMAT

Technical discussions and testing feedback with team Hazmat on modular frames, rapid deployment design, and competition-ready prototyping.

U-FORCE

Strategy, support, and collaboration with UAE-based team U-Force to exchange ideas and mechanisms.

G-FORCE

Cross-regional insights and brainstorming on outreach, hardware, and scoring innovations with team G-Force.

The Digital Error Collective
